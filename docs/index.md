# Welcome

## Building Tools to Support Urban Heat Management

This technical manual accompanies the heat-risk assessment and 3-30-300 analysis presented as part of a capstone project for the World Bank Group. The project was completed by Carnegie Mellon University students — Gabe Hafemann, Nandita Kannapadi, Nishan Sah, Sakina Mammadova, and Zoë Baker — under the guidance of faculty advisor Suzy Li.

The final presentation was delivered on **December 10, 2025**.
